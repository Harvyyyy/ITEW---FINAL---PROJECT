Zeus and the Titanomachy

Zeus, hidden from his father Cronus, rose to lead his siblings in a cosmic war against the Titans. Armed with thunderbolts forged by Cyclopes, he battled for control of Olympus, reshaping the divine order.

Clever Hermes crafted a lyre from a tortoise shell, enchanting Apollo with its music. His wit and guile earned him a place among the Olympians.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Greek mythology and our website.

Greek mythology is a collection of stories and legends from ancient Greece that explain the origins of the world, the lives and activities of deities, heroes, and mythological creatures. These myths were an integral part of ancient Greek religion and culture.

The Olympian Gods were the principal deities in Greek mythology who lived on Mount Olympus. They included Zeus (king of the gods), Hera, Poseidon, Demeter, Athena, Apollo, Artemis, Ares, Aphrodite, Hephaestus, Hermes, and either Hestia or Dionysus.

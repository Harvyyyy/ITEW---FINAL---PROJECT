Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Enchanted Realms. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your data when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Email addresses provided through our subscription form.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website, such as pages visited and links clicked, collected via cookies and analytics tools.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device information for site optimization and security.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Send you updates and newsletters if you subscribe.
  • Improve our website’s functionality and user experience.
  • Analyze site traffic and user behavior to enhance content.
  • Ensure the security of our website and prevent fraud.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party tools for analytics, email delivery, or hosting, who are bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law or to protect our rights.

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. These include:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for website functionality.
  • Analytics Cookies: To understand user behavior and improve our site.

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures, such as encryption and secure servers, to protect your data. However, no online system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access or request deletion of your personal data.
  • Unsubscribe from our newsletters at any time.
  • Contact us to update or correct your information.

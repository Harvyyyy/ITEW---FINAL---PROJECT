Zeus' Thunderbolt
Weapon of the Sky God

Forged by the Cyclopes, Zeus’ thunderbolt is a symbol of his supreme power, used to enforce order and punish those who defy the gods.

Symbols: Lightning, power

Associated With: Zeus, king of the gods; crafted by the Cyclopes.

Pandora's Box
Source of All Evils

Actually a jar, Pandora’s box was opened by the first woman, releasing all evils into the world, leaving only hope inside.

Symbols: Jar, hope

Associated With: Pandora, created by Hephaestus; gift from Zeus.

Athena's Aegis
Shield with Medusa’s Head

Athena’s aegis, a protective shield or breastplate often bearing Medusa’s head, symbolizes wisdom and divine protection in battle.

Symbols: Medusa, shield

Associated With: Athena, goddess of wisdom; sometimes lent to Zeus.

Hermes' Winged Sandals
Gift for Swift Travel

Hermes’ winged sandals, or talaria, granted him incredible speed, enabling his role as the gods’ swift messenger.

Symbols: Wings, speed

Associated With: Hermes, messenger god; gifted by Zeus or crafted by Hephaestus.
